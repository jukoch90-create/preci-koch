Who we are

Built on
Experience.

Preci & Koch Commodities was born from a proven track record in digital commodity trading. Our founder, Mr. Koch, served as Chief Operations Officer at DCX Platform — DCX.group — since 2019, where over 1 million tons of rice, corn, and fertilizers were traded internationally.

Our mission is to connect producers to buyers in a more direct, efficient, and transparent way — eliminating unnecessary intermediaries and reducing transaction risk through technology.

We specialize in yearly supply contracts and spot trades, with the flexibility to accommodate special orders including non-GMO products under producers' terms and conditions.
